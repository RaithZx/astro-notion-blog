## When Prompts Appear

### Smart Trigger System

The modal automatically appears based on user behavior:

| Trigger | When It Happens | Why This Timing |
|---------|----------------|-----------------|
| **30-Second Timer** | After user has been on the page for 30 seconds | Shows genuine interest in content |
| **50% Article Read** | When user scrolls halfway through an article | Engaged with content, likely enjoying it |
| **Exit Intent** | When mouse moves to close the tab (desktop only) | Last chance to capture their attention |

**Important:** Only ONE trigger fires per visit. Whichever happens first wins.

## FAQ for Stakeholders

**Q: Will this hurt our SEO?**  
A: No. Google doesn't penalize newsletter pop-ups if content is accessible.

**Q: Will users be annoyed?**  
A: Unlikely. The system is designed to be respectful and non-intrusive.

**Q: Can we turn it off?**  
A: Yes, anytime. Just stop the API service.

**Q: How do we measure success?**  
A: Track subscriber growth, open rates, and bounce rate (should not increase).

**Q: What if someone complains?**  
A: They can easily close the modal or unsubscribe from emails.

**Q: Is this legal in Europe?**  
A: Yes, fully GDPR compliant with explicit consent.

**Q: How is this different from a paywall?**  
A: Users can always close the prompt and read freely. No payment required.
